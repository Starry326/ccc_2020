COMP90024 Project 2

Goal:

To harvest tweets from across the regions of Australia on the UniMelb Research Cloud and undertake a variety of social media data analytics scenarios that tell intesting stories of life in Australian regions and importantly how the Twitter data can be used alongside/compared with/augment the data available within the AURIN platform to improve our knowledge of life in the regions of Australia.

  1. How many tweets mention nightclub or bar? Are these clusterd in certain areas?
  2. How many tweets mention covid-19 or coronavirus? Are these clusterd in certain areas?
  3. How many tweets mention covid-19, financial pressure or pressure? Are these clusterd in certain areas?
